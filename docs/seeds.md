# Exercise Seeds

3 Levels, 10 movements per level.

**Level: Novice**
1. Scaled Push Up
 - Elevate your hands or drop down to your knees
2. Assisted Pull Up
 - Find a stable platform (like a chair) to assist with a pull up
3. Assisted Dip
4. Mt Climber Burpee
5. Plank from the Knees
6. Air Squat
7. Good Morning
8. Lunge
9. Hip Raises
10. Step Up
11. Sit ups

**Level: Moderate**
1. Moderate Push Up
 - Elbow by your side, keep your core tight
2. Pull Up
3. Dip
4. Plank Burpee
5. Plank
6. Squat
7. Good Morning
8. Lunge
9. Weighted Hip Raises
10. Step Up

**Level: Advanced**
1. Advanced Push Up
 - Elevate your feet or add weight
2. Weighted Pull Up
3. Weighted Dip
4. Full Burpee
5. Elevated Plank
6. Jump Squat
7. Good Morning
8. Single Split Lunge with Hop
9. Weighted/Banded Hip Raises
10. Box Jump
